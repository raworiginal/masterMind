MASTERMIND

Guess the secret sequence of four colors in the correct order.

How to Play

  1. Click the color buttons to build your guess (colors can repeat).
  2. Use DELETE to remove the last color or CLEAR to reset your guess.
  3. Click SUBMIT to check your guess.
    • blue peg One choice in the correct position
    • pink peg One choice is a correct color but in the wrong position.
    • gray peg One choice is not in the sequence
  4. Use the clues to refine your next guess. You have 10 tries to crack the code!

Tip: Feedback pegs don't line up with specific color positions
